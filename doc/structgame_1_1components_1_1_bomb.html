Signed delta between the entity position and its main cell center.

Unsigned distance from the cell center to the opposite side of the entity (furthest from the center)

(centerDistance.x - 0.5f) is the distance overtaking on the next cell since the distance from the center of the cell to the start of an other is 0.5f (all cells are of size {1, 1}) We compare it with a % of the entity size to detect wether it is on the cell or not.
