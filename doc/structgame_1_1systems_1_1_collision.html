void game::systems::Collision::run ( ecs::SystemData  data)
finaloverridevirtual

Resolves the collisions of dynamics collidable entities.

Do not collide entities with themselves

If entity1 has a bomb no clip enabled and entity 2 is the ignored (static) bomb we ignore the collision.

Bomb related tests

Landmines explode on first collision

Kicked bomb(s)

Static bomb (can't be bomb1 because first entity must have a velocity)

Kick bomb if the player is moving toward it.

Implements ecs::System.
